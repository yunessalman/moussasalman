Buying with confidence

How Buying Works

Buying an original painting should feel as safe as it is special. That's why every purchase is handled through Escrow.com, a licensed, independent payment service trusted worldwide for high-value sales.

Here's what happens

  1. When you buy a painting, your payment is held securely by Escrow.com, not paid directly to me.

  2. Only once your payment is safely held do I carefully pack and ship your painting to you.

  3. When it arrives, you have a few days to unwrap it and confirm it's exactly as described.

  4. Your payment is released to me only after you're satisfied.

If anything isn't right, Escrow.com is there to help resolve it.

Every painting also comes with a signed certificate of authenticity, naming the work and confirming it as an original by my hand, so its provenance is yours to keep.

It's a simple, secure way to buy with complete peace of mind, wherever in the world you are.
