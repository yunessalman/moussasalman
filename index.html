The story

Self-taught,
led by feeling

Born in Cairo and based in Amsterdam, Moussa Salman came to art with no formal training. A former businessman, teacher and inventor who found his language in colour. His work is inspired by his daughter, born in 1999 with the rare chronic illness Epidermolysis Bullosa, and by the family's daily fight alongside her.
